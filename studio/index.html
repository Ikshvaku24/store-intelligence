How to use

  1. Pick a camera, then ⤵ Extract frames from its video.
  2. Choose a clear frame in the Frame dropdown.
  3. Click points around a shelf to draw a zone; fill the form & Save.
  4. Entry cameras: click p1, p2 across the door, then 1 point inside.
  5. Run the detection pipeline from the terminal (see README).
